Guardians (2/8)

King's Last Stand

Pac-Man-esque ghosts pour from the walls, and among them lurks a shapeshifting horror that splits in two each time it's struck down. Lead your King and his minion-like Royal Guardsmen — armed with fire pistols — to purge every last one, across 10 escalating levels. Level 1 is a gentle warm-up; by Level 10 the horde is relentless.

Mouse: Move Mouse to steer the King  |  Left Click (hold) fires the pistols  |  Right Click throws a bomb.
Touch: Drag anywhere on the battlefield to steer the King  |  🔫 button fires the pistols  |  💣 button throws a bomb.
The King's bomb lands where you aim (or at the nearest foe on touch) and explodes after 1 second, damaging every enemy caught in the blast.
Guardsmen are fragile — 3 hits from ghosts and they fall — but collect a glowing Guardian icon to recruit more, up to 8 guardians, a swirling Speed icon to double your guardians' fire rate, or a blue Shield icon to grant the King and every guardian 5 seconds of immunity while fully healing the guardians. Beware: the shapeshifting Monster kills a guardian in a single touch and grows stronger from it.
The King has only 3 lives each level. Lose them all and the kingdom falls.
